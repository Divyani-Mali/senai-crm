# SenAI Pricing Policy

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan

- Price: $29/month
- Users: Up to 5
- Features: Basic email management, standard support, 1000 API calls/month
- Storage: 10GB

### Standard Plan

- Price: $99/month
- Users: Up to 25
- Features: Advanced analytics, priority support, 10,000 API calls/month
- Storage: 100GB
- SLA: 99.5% uptime guarantee

### Professional Plan

- Price: $299/month
- Users: Up to 100
- Features: Full feature set, dedicated support, 100,000 API calls/month
- Storage: 500GB
- SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee

### Enterprise Plan

- Price: Custom pricing (contact sales)
- Users: Unlimited
- Features: Custom integrations, SLA guarantees, dedicated infrastructure
- Storage: Unlimited
- SLA: 99.99% uptime guarantee

## Special Discounts

### Non-Profit Discount

- 30% discount on Standard plan
- Requires 501(c)(3) or equivalent documentation
- Applied automatically after verification (3-5 business days)
- Cannot be combined with other discounts

### Annual Billing Discount

- 20% discount when paying annually
- Available on all plans except Enterprise
- Pro-rata refund available within first 30 days

### Startup Discount

- 50% off for first 6 months
- Eligible: Companies under 2 years old, under $1M ARR
- Requires verification

## Pro-Rata Billing Rules

- Mid-cycle upgrades: charged pro-rata from upgrade date
- Mid-cycle downgrades: credited pro-rata to next invoice
- Example: Upgrade from Standard to Pro on day 15 of 30-day cycle = 50% of price difference charged
- Enterprise custom contracts: billed as per contract terms

## Payment Terms

- All plans: Payment due on 1st of each month
- Enterprise: Net-30 invoice terms available
- Failed payments: 3 retry attempts over 7 days, then service suspension
- Reactivation fee: $25 after suspension
