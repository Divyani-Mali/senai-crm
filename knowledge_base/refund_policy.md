# SenAI Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Policy

- Refunds available within 14 days of purchase only
- After 14 days: No cash refunds, credits only
- Refund processing time: 5-7 business days
- Refund method: Original payment method only

## Exception Process

- Medical emergency: Full refund regardless of timeline (documentation required)
- Billing error by SenAI: Full refund within 24 hours
- Service never delivered: Full refund regardless of timeline
- Exception requests: support@senai.io with subject "Refund Exception Request"

## Credits vs Refunds

- Credits: Instant, applied to account, never expire
- Refunds: 5-7 days, only within 14-day window
- Credits recommended for: Upgrades, plan changes, goodwill gestures
- Credits value: 110% of refund amount (10% bonus for accepting credits)

## Churn Retention Playbook

- Step 1: Acknowledge frustration, apologize sincerely
- Step 2: Offer 1 month free credit immediately
- Step 3: If still unhappy: offer 3 months free + plan downgrade
- Step 4: If threatening public review: escalate to Customer Success Manager
- Step 5: VIP accounts threatening churn: escalate to VP of Customer Success immediately
- Never argue with customer about validity of complaint
- Always offer a goodwill gesture regardless of fault

## Subscription Cancellation

- Cancel anytime, no penalty
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Data retained for 90 days after cancellation
- Data export available during retention period
