# SenAI Escalation Matrix

## Security Incidents
- Ransomware / extortion threats: IMMEDIATELY escalate to security@senai.io + cto@senai.io
- Suspicious login alerts: security@senai.io within 15 minutes
- Data breach threats: security@senai.io + legal@senai.io + cto@senai.io
- NEVER auto-reply to security threats
- NEVER negotiate with ransomware attackers

## Legal Threats
- Cease and desist letters: legal@senai.io within 1 hour
- SLA breach + legal threat: legal@senai.io + ceo@senai.io
- GDPR formal requests: legal@senai.io + compliance@senai.io immediately
- Class action threats: legal@senai.io + ceo@senai.io + pr@senai.io
- Do NOT make any commitments or admissions in automated replies

## VIP Customer Escalations
- VIP account threatening churn: vp-success@senai.io within 2 hours
- Enterprise account (>$10k MRR) complaint: account-manager@senai.io within 1 hour
- Public review threat: customer-success@senai.io + pr@senai.io
- Reputation crisis (3+ unanswered complaints): escalate to VP Customer Success immediately

## PR Crisis
- Negative press inquiry: pr@senai.io within 30 minutes
- Social media viral complaint: pr@senai.io + ceo@senai.io
- Competitor attacking publicly: pr@senai.io

## P0 Technical Incidents
- Complete service outage: engineering@senai.io + cto@senai.io + status-page update
- Data loss incident: cto@senai.io + legal@senai.io + affected customers notified within 1 hour
- Security breach confirmed: Full incident response team activated

## Compliance
- HIPAA violation report: compliance@senai.io + legal@senai.io immediately
- GDPR Article 20 request: legal@senai.io + compliance@senai.io, create ticket, acknowledge within 24h
- Audit request from regulator: legal@senai.io + ceo@senai.io immediately

## Churn Risk
- Sentiment score below -0.6 for 3+ consecutive emails: auto-escalate to customer-success@senai.io
- No reply to customer for 48+ hours: alert assigned team member
- Customer mentions competitor: flag to sales@senai.io
