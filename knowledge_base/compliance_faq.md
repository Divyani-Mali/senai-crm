# SenAI Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA Compliance

- HIPAA BAA (Business Associate Agreement) available on Professional and Enterprise plans
- BAA request: compliance@senai.io with subject "HIPAA BAA Request"
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- HIPAA-compliant data storage available in US-East and US-West regions only

## GDPR Compliance

- SenAI is fully GDPR compliant as of May 2018
- DPA (Data Processing Agreement) available for all paid plans
- DPA request: privacy@senai.io
- Data residency options: EU-West (Ireland), EU-Central (Frankfurt)

## GDPR Data Subject Rights

- Article 20 (Data Portability): Must respond within 30 days, data provided in machine-readable format
- Article 17 (Right to Erasure): Must respond within 30 days
- Article 15 (Right of Access): Must respond within 30 days
- All GDPR requests: Immediately flag to legal@senai.io and create compliance ticket
- GDPR requests must NOT be handled with generic auto-replies
- Statutory deadline: 30 days from receipt of request

## SOC 2 Type II

- SenAI holds SOC 2 Type II certification
- Last audit: Q4 2024
- Report available under NDA for Enterprise customers
- Request: security@senai.io

## Data Residency Options

- US-East (Virginia) - default
- US-West (Oregon)
- EU-West (Ireland) - GDPR preferred
- EU-Central (Frankfurt) - GDPR preferred
- APAC (Singapore)
- Enterprise only: Custom dedicated region

## Security Standards

- Encryption at rest: AES-256
- Encryption in transit: TLS 1.3
- Penetration testing: Quarterly
- Bug bounty program: security@senai.io
