# SenAI SLA Policy

## Uptime Guarantee

- Starter Plan: 99.5% monthly uptime
- Standard Plan: 99.9% monthly uptime
- Professional Plan: 99.9% monthly uptime
- Enterprise Plan: 99.99% monthly uptime

## Downtime Definition

- Downtime: Service unavailable for >1 minute
- Scheduled maintenance: NOT counted as downtime
- Maintenance windows: Sundays 2AM-4AM UTC (advance notice 48h)

## Incident Response Times

- P0 (Complete outage): Response within 15 minutes, update every 30 minutes
- P1 (Major degradation): Response within 1 hour, update every 2 hours
- P2 (Minor issues): Response within 4 hours, update every 8 hours
- P3 (Low impact): Response within 24 hours

## SLA Credit Calculation

- 99.9% - 99.5% uptime: 10% credit of monthly bill
- 99.5% - 99.0% uptime: 25% credit of monthly bill
- Below 99.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly bill
- Below 95.0% uptime: 100% credit of monthly bill

## Credit Claim Process

- Must be claimed within 30 days of incident
- Submit via support ticket with incident reference
- Credits applied to next invoice (not cash refunds)
- Maximum credit: 100% of monthly bill

## RCA (Root Cause Analysis) Delivery

- P0 incidents: RCA delivered within 24 hours
- P1 incidents: RCA delivered within 72 hours
- RCA includes: Timeline, root cause, fix applied, prevention measures

## Legal Escalation

- SLA breach + legal threat: Immediately escalate to legal@senai.io
- Do not make commitments beyond documented SLA credits
- All legal communications must go through legal team
